    This charming modern west coast designed duplex is an architecturally distinct place to call home - designed and built with families and livability in mind. 3 beds up with primary en-suite, additional full bath up, and a powder on the main! Wide plank flooring throughout all living areas, porcelain tile in baths, quartz kitchen countertops, premium Bosch appliances, Bloomberg washer and dryer, radiant heat, wired security system, private porch off main entrance & second BBQ patio off dining area. Quiet, treelined, family friendly street close to markets, restaurants, parks, shops, schools, and transit in one of Vancouver’s most walkable neighborhoods - and less than a 7 minute drive from the Downtown Core. Like new BUT NO GST!!! Open Thur June 8th from 5-6pm and Sat/Sun from 2-4pm
